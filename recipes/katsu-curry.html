Katsu Curry

Description

Katsu curry is a Japanese dish consisting of a pork cutlet served with a portion of japanese rice and curry.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 300ml chicken stock
  • 100ml coconut milk
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 500g chicken
  • panko bread crumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • Rice

Steps:

  1. Firstly, make the curry sauce. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan, cook the onions, garlic and ginger until they are soft and start to caramelise.
  2. Add the curry powder and turmeric. Stir well and let it cook for a min on low/medium heat.
  3. Add flour to thicken and cook for 1 min.
  4. Gently add the chicken stock mix with water and stir continuously while adding the stock mix.
  5. When the curry starts to boil add some coconut milk to the mix and keep stirring.
  6. Lastly, add some sugar and soy sauce and let it simmer for 20 mins.
  7. In a wide bowl, mix the cornflour with 4 tbsp water and some seasoning.
  8. Dip the chicken into the four mixture.
  9. Place the breadcrumbs in another bowl and dip the chicken in it, turning until well coated.
  10. Heat the oil in a frying pan and cook the chicken for 4-5 mins on each side until golden and cooked through.
